JUDGE PROVENANCE — every AblationReport JSON in this directory was scored with rubric-v1
(pre-#83): the UNWEIGHTED mean of the five RubricJudge dimensions. Main uses rubric-v2 (#83):
a factuality-weighted headline (factuality weight 0.5, validity flag, factuality<=0.1 clamp),
which scores strictly LOWER on identical predictions (~0.58 where v1 scored ~0.70). Re-running
the reproduce commands on main will produce lower absolute numbers; the cross-condition
comparisons (which config/count beats which) are what these results are read for.

Serve+judge models: trace_scaling_results/ = Opus 4.8; rag_opt_results/ & rag_topk_results/ =
mixed Opus 4.8 (top_k A/B) and Sonnet 4.6 (opt-vs-unopt, embedder/key A/B, factuality, contam).
